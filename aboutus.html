What is TransitAura?

TransitAura provides real-time safety insights and easy access to emergency contacts. empowering transit users to make safe, informed choices while using public transportation.

Our Story

TransitAura began with our mission to make public transit safer and more accessible for everyone.

Our Values

Our core values guide our approach:

  • User Safety: We are committed to providing features that prioritize the safety and well-being of transit users.
  • Accessibility: We believe in making transit information accessible to all, fostering a connected and informed community.
  • Community Focus: Our app is designed to serve transit riders by helping create a network of safer and more informed travelers.

Our Vision for Transit Safety

Every transit user deserves peace of mind when they travel. TransitAura was built with this belief at its core, helping communities stay informed of potential safety concerns and fostering a safer environment for everyone.

Meet the Team

We are three dedicated Computer Systems Technology students with a passion for making a difference. Our fresh perspectives and shared commitment to public safety have driven us to create an app that empowers transit users to stay informed, safe, and connected.

Why We Built TransitAura: We saw an opportunity to improve transit experiences by addressing safety concerns. With insights from our coursework and our own transit experiences, we aimed to bridge this gap with a user-friendly, responsive app.
